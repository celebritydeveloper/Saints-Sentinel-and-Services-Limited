Saints Sentinel and Services Limited is a security support services company founded in November 2016. The Company commenced operations in Lagos in January 2017 and has since expanded operations to different parts of the Country.
Saints Sentinel provides security support services to government security agencies, security companies, and the security departments of agencies, organisations and corporations. The support services include training, intelligence and surveillance support and security personnel management.

Our C.E.O

Chinazo Bertrand Chikeziri is an ex-naval special forces officer. He retired from the Nigerian Navy on 31 December 2016 as a Lieutenant Commander after serving for more than 10 years in the Nigerian Navy Special Boat Service 3 (NNSBS), the Naval Special Forces Formation.
Chikeziri attended various foreign and local courses which enhanced hisproficiency as a Special Forces officer and a security operative. He was trained by US Army Special Forces, US Navy SEALs, US Navy and US Coast Guard. He was also trained by UK Special Forces and Army, and by Nigerian DSS.
