About Guardian Angel

Security is an essential requirement in all aspects of life’s endeavours, farming is not an exception. In order to maximize productivity in farming, it is necessary to prevent loss due to stealing, misuse of materials or damage, whether intentional or inadvertent. It is also necessary to safe guard the people working on these farms. Guardian Angel provides products and services which ensure the security of human and material assets on a farm, and prevent all forms of losses.

Guardian Angel Services is a joint venture of 3 security and support services companies, Saints Sentinel & Services, Top-Private Security Services and Modern Geeks IT Limited. Guardian Angel is a market leader in farm security and loss prevention services. With the application of cutting edge technology, we provide a holistic solution for all security threats to a farm; physical, personnel or biological.
